\section{Introduction}

Ethos manages identity for humans and AI agents. This specification
models the organizational layer: how identities are assigned to roles,
how roles form teams, and how teams are scoped to repositories.

An \emph{identity} is a person or agent with stable attributes ---
personality, writing style, talents. An identity exists independently
of any team.

A \emph{role} is a function within an organization --- COO, Go
Specialist, Security Engineer. Roles have responsibilities and
collaboration relationships with other roles.

A \emph{team} is a named collection that binds identities to roles for
a set of repositories. The same identity can hold different roles on
different teams. The same role type can be filled by multiple
identities on the same team.

\section{Basic Types}

\begin{zed}
  [HANDLE, NAME, SLUG, REPOPATH]
\end{zed}

$HANDLE$ identifies an identity uniquely (e.g., ``claude'', ``bwk'').
$NAME$ is a display name or role name. $SLUG$ references attribute
files (personalities, writing styles, talents). $REPOPATH$ identifies
a repository.

\section{Free Types}

\begin{zed}
  Kind ::= human | agent
\end{zed}

\begin{zed}
  CollabType ::= reportsto | collabswith | delegatesto
\end{zed}

$CollabType$ describes the nature of a collaboration relationship
between two roles: hierarchical reporting, peer collaboration, or
delegation of work.

\section{Global Constants}

\begin{axdef}
  maxIdent : \nat \\
  maxRoles : \nat \\
  maxTeams : \nat \\
  maxMembers : \nat \\
  maxCollabs : \nat \\
  maxTalents : \nat \\
  maxRepos : \nat
\where
  maxIdent = 3 \\
  maxRoles = 3 \\
  maxTeams = 2 \\
  maxMembers = 5 \\
  maxCollabs = 3 \\
  maxTalents = 3 \\
  maxRepos = 3
\end{axdef}

\section{Assignment and Collaboration Schemas}

An assignment binds an identity to a role on a team.

\begin{schema}{Assignment}
  aTeam : NAME \\
  aHandle : HANDLE \\
  aRole : NAME
\end{schema}

A collaboration is a directed relationship between two roles on
the same team.

\begin{schema}{Collab}
  cTeam : NAME \\
  cFrom : NAME \\
  cTo : NAME \\
  cType : CollabType
\where
  cFrom \neq cTo
\end{schema}

A role cannot collaborate with itself.

\section{System State}

The system state holds all identities, roles, teams, and their
relationships.

\begin{schema}{EthosOrg}
  known\_handles : \finset HANDLE \\
  id\_name : HANDLE \pfun NAME \\
  id\_kind : HANDLE \pfun Kind \\
  id\_personality : HANDLE \pfun SLUG \\
  id\_writing\_style : HANDLE \pfun SLUG \\
  known\_roles : \finset NAME \\
  team\_names : \finset NAME \\
  team\_repos : NAME \pfun \finset REPOPATH \\
  members : \finset Assignment \\
  collabs : \finset Collab
\where
  \# known\_handles \leq maxIdent \\
  \# known\_roles \leq maxRoles \\
  \# team\_names \leq maxTeams \\
  \# members \leq maxMembers \\
  \# collabs \leq maxCollabs \\
  \forall tn : team\_names @ \# (team\_repos~tn) \leq maxRepos \\
  \dom id\_name = known\_handles \\
  \dom id\_kind = known\_handles \\
  \dom id\_personality = known\_handles \\
  \dom id\_writing\_style = known\_handles \\
  \dom team\_repos = team\_names \\
  \forall m : members @ \\
  \quad m.aTeam \in team\_names \land \\
  \quad m.aHandle \in known\_handles \land \\
  \quad m.aRole \in known\_roles \\
  \forall c : collabs @ \\
  \quad c.cTeam \in team\_names \land \\
  \quad c.cFrom \in known\_roles \land \\
  \quad c.cTo \in known\_roles \land \\
  \quad (\exists m1 : members @ m1.aTeam = c.cTeam \land m1.aRole = c.cFrom) \land \\
  \quad (\exists m2 : members @ m2.aTeam = c.cTeam \land m2.aRole = c.cTo) \\
  \forall tn : team\_names @ \\
  \quad (\exists m : members @ m.aTeam = tn)
\end{schema}

Key invariants:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item Handle uniqueness: $known\_handles$ is a set, maps are keyed by handle.
  \item Role name uniqueness: $known\_roles$ is a set.
  \item Referential integrity: every assignment references a valid
    team name, identity handle, and role name.
  \item Non-empty teams: every team name has at least one member.
  \item Collaboration validity: both roles in a collaboration are
    filled by members on that team.
  \item No self-collaboration: $cFrom \neq cTo$ (schema invariant).
  \item Bounded cardinality: all collections bounded by global constants.
\end{enumerate}

\section{Operations}

\subsection{Create Identity}

\begin{schema}{CreateIdentity}
  \Delta EthosOrg \\
  h? : HANDLE \\
  n? : NAME \\
  k? : Kind \\
  p? : SLUG \\
  ws? : SLUG
\where
  h? \notin known\_handles \\
  \# known\_handles < maxIdent \\
  known\_handles' = known\_handles \cup \{ h? \} \\
  id\_name' = id\_name \cup \{ h? \mapsto n? \} \\
  id\_kind' = id\_kind \cup \{ h? \mapsto k? \} \\
  id\_personality' = id\_personality \cup \{ h? \mapsto p? \} \\
  id\_writing\_style' = id\_writing\_style \cup \{ h? \mapsto ws? \} \\
  known\_roles' = known\_roles \\
  team\_names' = team\_names \\
  team\_repos' = team\_repos \\
  members' = members \\
  collabs' = collabs
\end{schema}

\subsection{Create Role}

\begin{schema}{CreateRole}
  \Delta EthosOrg \\
  rn? : NAME
\where
  rn? \notin known\_roles \\
  \# known\_roles < maxRoles \\
  known\_roles' = known\_roles \cup \{ rn? \} \\
  known\_handles' = known\_handles \\
  id\_name' = id\_name \\
  id\_kind' = id\_kind \\
  id\_personality' = id\_personality \\
  id\_writing\_style' = id\_writing\_style \\
  team\_names' = team\_names \\
  team\_repos' = team\_repos \\
  members' = members \\
  collabs' = collabs
\end{schema}

\subsection{Create Team}

A team is created with a name, a set of repositories, and a first
member (maintaining the non-empty invariant).

\begin{schema}{CreateTeam}
  \Delta EthosOrg \\
  tn? : NAME \\
  repos? : \finset REPOPATH \\
  fh? : HANDLE \\
  fr? : NAME
\where
  tn? \notin team\_names \\
  \# team\_names < maxTeams \\
  \# repos? \leq maxRepos \\
  fh? \in known\_handles \\
  fr? \in known\_roles \\
  team\_names' = team\_names \cup \{ tn? \} \\
  team\_repos' = team\_repos \cup \{ tn? \mapsto repos? \} \\
  members' = members \cup \{ (\mu Assignment | \\
  \quad aTeam = tn? \land aHandle = fh? \land aRole = fr?) \} \\
  known\_handles' = known\_handles \\
  id\_name' = id\_name \\
  id\_kind' = id\_kind \\
  id\_personality' = id\_personality \\
  id\_writing\_style' = id\_writing\_style \\
  known\_roles' = known\_roles \\
  collabs' = collabs
\end{schema}

\subsection{Add Member}

\begin{schema}{AddMember}
  \Delta EthosOrg \\
  tn? : NAME \\
  h? : HANDLE \\
  rn? : NAME
\where
  tn? \in team\_names \\
  h? \in known\_handles \\
  rn? \in known\_roles \\
  \# members < maxMembers \\
  \lnot (\exists m : members @ \\
  \quad m.aTeam = tn? \land m.aHandle = h? \land m.aRole = rn?) \\
  members' = members \cup \{ (\mu Assignment | \\
  \quad aTeam = tn? \land aHandle = h? \land aRole = rn?) \} \\
  known\_handles' = known\_handles \\
  id\_name' = id\_name \\
  id\_kind' = id\_kind \\
  id\_personality' = id\_personality \\
  id\_writing\_style' = id\_writing\_style \\
  known\_roles' = known\_roles \\
  team\_names' = team\_names \\
  team\_repos' = team\_repos \\
  collabs' = collabs
\end{schema}

\subsection{Remove Member}

\begin{schema}{RemoveMember}
  \Delta EthosOrg \\
  tn? : NAME \\
  h? : HANDLE \\
  rn? : NAME
\where
  tn? \in team\_names \\
  (\exists m : members @ \\
  \quad m.aTeam = tn? \land m.aHandle = h? \land m.aRole = rn?) \\
  \# \{ m : members | m.aTeam = tn? \} > 1 \\
  members' = \{ m : members | \\
  \quad \lnot (m.aTeam = tn? \land m.aHandle = h? \land m.aRole = rn?) \} \\
  collabs' = \{ c : collabs | \\
  \quad \lnot (c.cTeam = tn? \land (c.cFrom = rn? \lor c.cTo = rn?)) \} \\
  known\_handles' = known\_handles \\
  id\_name' = id\_name \\
  id\_kind' = id\_kind \\
  id\_personality' = id\_personality \\
  id\_writing\_style' = id\_writing\_style \\
  known\_roles' = known\_roles \\
  team\_names' = team\_names \\
  team\_repos' = team\_repos
\end{schema}

Cannot remove the last member. Removing a member also removes any
collaborations involving their role on this team.

\subsection{Add Collaboration}

\begin{schema}{AddCollaboration}
  \Delta EthosOrg \\
  tn? : NAME \\
  from? : NAME \\
  to? : NAME \\
  ct? : CollabType
\where
  tn? \in team\_names \\
  from? \neq to? \\
  from? \in known\_roles \\
  to? \in known\_roles \\
  \# collabs < maxCollabs \\
  (\exists m1 : members @ m1.aTeam = tn? \land m1.aRole = from?) \\
  (\exists m2 : members @ m2.aTeam = tn? \land m2.aRole = to?) \\
  collabs' = collabs \cup \{ (\mu Collab | \\
  \quad cTeam = tn? \land cFrom = from? \land cTo = to? \land cType = ct?) \} \\
  known\_handles' = known\_handles \\
  id\_name' = id\_name \\
  id\_kind' = id\_kind \\
  id\_personality' = id\_personality \\
  id\_writing\_style' = id\_writing\_style \\
  known\_roles' = known\_roles \\
  team\_names' = team\_names \\
  team\_repos' = team\_repos \\
  members' = members
\end{schema}

Both roles must be filled by members on the team.

\subsection{Add Repository to Team}

\begin{schema}{AddRepository}
  \Delta EthosOrg \\
  tn? : NAME \\
  repo? : REPOPATH
\where
  tn? \in team\_names \\
  \# (team\_repos~tn?) < maxRepos \\
  team\_repos' = team\_repos \oplus \\
  \quad \{ tn? \mapsto (team\_repos~tn?) \cup \{ repo? \} \} \\
  known\_handles' = known\_handles \\
  id\_name' = id\_name \\
  id\_kind' = id\_kind \\
  id\_personality' = id\_personality \\
  id\_writing\_style' = id\_writing\_style \\
  known\_roles' = known\_roles \\
  team\_names' = team\_names \\
  members' = members \\
  collabs' = collabs
\end{schema}

\section{System Invariants Summary}

\begin{enumerate}
  \item Handle uniqueness: $known\_handles$ is a set, identity maps
    are keyed by handle.
  \item Role name uniqueness: $known\_roles$ is a set.
  \item Team name uniqueness: $team\_names$ is a set, $team\_repos$
    maps each name to its repos.
  \item Referential integrity: every assignment references a valid
    team name, identity handle, and role name.
  \item Non-empty teams: every team name has at least one member.
  \item Collaboration validity: both roles in a collaboration are
    filled by members on that team.
  \item No self-collaboration: $cFrom \neq cTo$.
  \item Bounded cardinality: all collections bounded by global constants.
\end{enumerate}
